What LUXE is
A wholesale brand built for the people who install, not the people who scroll.

LUXE sells its own brand of paint protection and window film through an approved, account-gated dealer network, so pricing stays visible only to installers, shops, and distributors. We own the brand instead of reselling someone else's, and product ships today from our base in Union City, California. Founded 2023.
